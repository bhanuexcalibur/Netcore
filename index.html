I’m only going on what’s public here, not an internal audit, but the gap in visible surface area is the thing this plan is built to close.

Why this, not just a press release

A buyer evaluating a CDP or an ESP doesn’t read the press release. They see Netcore’s own landing page during a Google search three months from now, or a line in a competitor’s battlecard, or a slide in a live sales cycle. The recognition is only as valuable as the slowest asset it reaches. That’s a project management problem before it’s a messaging problem, which is why the plan above starts with owners and dates, not headlines.
